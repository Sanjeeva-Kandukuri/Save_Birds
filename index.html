Technologies That Harm Birds

Mobile Tower Radiation

Electromagnetic radiation from mobile towers affects bird navigation and their reproductive systems.

Glass Buildings

Birds collide with glass buildings because they cannot recognize reflective surfaces.

Noise Pollution

Heavy city noise disturbs bird communication and breeding behavior.

Deforestation

Urban development destroys forests which are natural homes for birds.

Technology Solutions to Protect Birds

AI Bird Monitoring

AI cameras track bird populations and help scientists protect endangered species.

Drone Monitoring

Drones help monitor forests and bird habitats without disturbing wildlife.

Smart Bird Feeders

IoT feeders provide food and water during extreme weather conditions.

Solar Water Stations

Solar powered water stations help birds survive during extreme summer heat.

Technology vs Bird Life

Modern technology has improved human life but it has also created many dangers for birds such as radiation, pollution, and habitat destruction.

Our mission is to use technology responsibly and protect birds by spreading awareness and building eco-friendly solutions.
